Hi! I’m Alfonso, and welcome to my video games portfolio!

This page is a showcase of the work I have been doing as a game developer. You will find all kind of projects and a blog to follow my work/personal progress.

Ronin-Hood Sisters

Tactical RPG with elements of Rogue-Lite where the player takes control of a group of magical girls with the ability to combine elements using their special powers that come from their costumes.

Medieval Floor

Four-player cooperative game with different roles whose goal is to eliminate endless waves of enemies and obtain the highest possible score.
Unity Zombie Killer

Shooter whose objective is to kill zombies and complete a mission with several objectives in the shortest possible time and with the highets possible number of eliminations.

Planet System Simulator 2D/3D

Project of a research group where the physical behavior of the trajectories of the different stars of planetary systems is simulated.

AlienMist

Prototype of a game based on Portal. Includes simple physics simulation and non-euclidean geometry. It is a shooter where different projectiles and portals are used to solve challenges.
