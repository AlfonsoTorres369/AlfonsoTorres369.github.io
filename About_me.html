Hi! I’m Alfonso, and I'm a Game Programmer!

Seccion descripcion y biblia

Since I was a child I have always been curious about "how" everyday devices work. In particular, all those related to the field of computing.

As usual, what surprise & interested me the most were video games, which for me were the closest thing to what could be considered as "magic". My desires went beyond wating to entertain or escape, whenever I played anything I always had the same question in mind: "How will it work?"

I loved everything related to video games but I wanted to give life to those characters, to those enemies, to those abilities... In short, I wanted to make those worlds "work" & transmit those feelings that we all feel when we play our favorite video games...

That's how i set out to become a video game programmer!

I'm a selft-taught, organized, team-oriented & highly motivated person capable of adapting to any project.

My main goal is to evolve in the videogame development sector as a programmer.
