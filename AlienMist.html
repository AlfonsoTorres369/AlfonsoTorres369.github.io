AlienMist is a TFG desgined & developed for the degree Software Engineering of the UPM. This videogame is a futuristic shooter based on puzzles that also incoporates portals, making the virtual world a non-euclidean world. In summary, the player must use the elements projectiles & the objects of the scenarios to be able to complete the challenges & advance to the next levels. Below, you can dowload the game.
