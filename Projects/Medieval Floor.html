Summary

Medieval Floor is a local cooperative game in which you will have to overcome infinite waves of enemies.

Groups of enemies will appear succesively & must be defeated to defend a small village & its habitants.

To achieve this, the attacks & abilities of the different characters must be perfectly coordinated.

PLAY MEDIEVAL FLOOR!
Cooperative game!

There are four characters available that must be mastered to defeat all the enemies.

Each of these characters must be controlled by a different player.

Coordination between players is esssential to survive as long as possible.

Different types of characters!

There are four characters available that must be mastered to survive.

Each of these characters has a specific role within the team & has a specific active skill.

  • Archer (Top Left): DPS Range & increase damage as special ability.

  • Warrior (Top Right): DPS Melee & increase defense as special ability.

  • Healer (Bottom Left): Support Melee & healing as special ability.

  • Wizard (Bottom Right): DPS Range & increase movement speed as special ability.

Various enemies!

The village will be attacked by enemies of different types & behaviors that force to prioritize objectives.

  • Archer: high damage enemy that attacks from great distances.

  • Armored Warrior: slow enemy but with a lot of health & very damaging attacks.

  • Warrior: balanced DPS enemy.

  • Rogue: enemy with low health that flees after attacking.
