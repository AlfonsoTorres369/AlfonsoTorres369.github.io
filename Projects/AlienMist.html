Summary

AlienMist is a prototype based on Portal and Portal 2 games.

It consists of three leves with different challenges or puzzles. To overcome them you have to use portals, projectiles and different types of machines and mechanisms.

PLAY ALIENMIST!

Non-Euclidean Geometry!

The most importante feature is the implementation of portals. An entity that connects two different points of the scenario without being physically linked. They allow bot the player and the projectiles to be teleported.

Physical alterations!

There are physical fields that influence the projectiles of the prototype.

The magnetic field, which forces the direction of the projectiles and the electric field that gives more speed or destroys or changes the projectiles.

Combination of projectiles!

The projectiles can be combined into one device. These allow only a single recipe, which when entered correctly will trigger some interesting mechanism.
