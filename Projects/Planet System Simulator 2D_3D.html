Summary

Planet System Simulator 2D/3D is an application that allows you to observe the behavior of some systems formed by stars and planets.

These simulations are infinite, but the systems will not be stable indefinitely, there will come a time when the system collapses and the stars lose their attraction between them.

PLAY PLANET SYSTEM SIMULATOR!

Simulation of the movement of stars!

Each of the planetary systems has its own behavior based on the masses of its stars and the proximity between them.

Different planetary systems!

Up to five different types of planetary systems can be simulated.

In addition, some of these typologies have some variations to observe the change of behavior betweem them.

Various tools and utilities!

The application contains some functionalities that make it more interesting.

For example, you can enable trails to the stars, rotate the camera freely, zoom in and out, skip iterations, slow down the simulation...
